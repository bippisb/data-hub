About the Data Hub

The Data Hub at Bharti Institute of Public Policy

The Data Hub is a strategic initiative by the Bharti Institute of Public Policy (BIPP) at the Indian School of Business (ISB) to centralise and streamline data-related functions across all research projects and initiatives.

By establishing a Central Data Team, we aim to transform how data is managed, analysed, and utilised not just within BIPP, but across ISB and beyond, creating a unified platform for public data suppor in policy and research.

How the Data Hub Supports ISB and Beyond

The Data Hub serves as a centralized resource for public data-related needs across departments, research centers in ISB, and external stakeholders. By establishing a dedicated Central Data Team, we provide:

For ISB Faculty & Researchers
  • Data Discovery & Access: Quick access to curated datasets relevant to specific research domain
  • Technical Expertise: Support from data engineers and scientists for complex data processes
  • Custom Solutions: Development of specialized tools and dashboards for your research
  • Grant Support: Data management plans and technical sections for funding proposals
For External Stakeholders
  • Policy Analytics: Data-driven insights for government agencies and policymakers
  • Custom Development: Tailored software solutions for data collection and analysis
  • Capacity Building: Training and knowledge transfer for partner organizations
  • Collaborative Research: Joint projects leveraging ISB's academic expertise
  • Data Standards: Support in implementation of best practices in data governance
Data Hub Services
Data Management
  • Data acquisition & curation
  • Data Warehousing
  • Data Documentation
  • Data visualization
Technical Development
  • API development
  • Dashboard creation
  • Custom tailored data applications

High-Value Datasets: A Foundation for Data Support

Our High-Value Datasets Repository represents the Data Hub's first major initiative in supporting the ISB community with accessible, well-documented data resources. It serves as a starting point for our broader mission of comprehensive public data support.

By providing centralized access to these carefully curated datasets, we aim to:

  • Accelerate Research: Enable researchers to quickly find and access relevant data without duplicating acquisition efforts
  • Ensure Quality: Maintain consistent documentation and metadata standards across all datasets
  • Foster Collaboration: Create opportunities for cross-departmental research through shared data resources
  • Build Capacity: Demonstrate the value of centralized data management to the ISB community
