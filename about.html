About the Data Hub

The Data Hub Initiative at BIPP

The Data Hub is a strategic initiative by the Bharti Institute of Public Policy (BIPP) at the Indian School of Business (ISB) to centralize and streamline data-related functions across all research projects and initiatives.

By establishing a Central Data Team, we aim to transform how data is managed, analyzed, and utilized not just within BIPP, but across ISB and beyond, creating a unified platform for data suppor in policy and research.

How the Data Hub Supports ISB and Beyond

The Data Hub serves as a centralized resource for all data-related needs across ISB departments, research centers, and external partners. By establishing a dedicated Central Data Team, we provide:

For ISB Faculty & Researchers
  • Data Discovery & Access: Quick access to curated datasets relevant to your research domain
  • Technical Expertise: Support from data engineers and scientists for complex analyses
  • Infrastructure Sharing: Access to centralized computing resources and storage
  • Custom Solutions: Development of specialized tools and dashboards for your research
  • Grant Support: Data management plans and technical sections for funding proposals
For External Partners
  • Policy Analytics: Data-driven insights for government agencies and policymakers
  • Custom Development: Tailored software solutions for data collection and analysis
  • Capacity Building: Training and knowledge transfer for partner organizations
  • Collaborative Research: Joint projects leveraging ISB's academic expertise
  • Data Standards: Implementation of best practices in data governance

High-Value Datasets: A Foundation for Data Support

This High-Value Datasets Repository represents the Data Hub's first major initiative in supporting the ISB community with accessible, well-documented data resources. It serves as a starting point for our broader mission of comprehensive data support.

By providing centralized access to these carefully curated datasets, we aim to:

  • Accelerate Research: Enable researchers to quickly find and access relevant data without duplicating acquisition efforts
  • Ensure Quality: Maintain consistent documentation and metadata standards across all datasets
  • Foster Collaboration: Create opportunities for cross-departmental research through shared data resources
  • Build Capacity: Demonstrate the value of centralized data management to the ISB community
