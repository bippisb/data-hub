About the Data Hub

Bharti Institute of Public Policy (BIPP)

The Bharti Institute of Public Policy (BIPP) at the Indian School of Business (ISB) is a leading research institute dedicated to conducting rigorous, evidence-based research to inform and influence public policy in India and beyond.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic research and policy practice by providing policymakers, researchers, and civil society with access to high-quality data and analysis that can drive meaningful change.

The High-Value Datasets Initiative

The High-Value Datasets initiative is a comprehensive effort to identify, curate, and make accessible datasets that have significant potential for research and policy analysis. These datasets span multiple sectors including:

  • Agriculture and Rural Development
  • Education and Human Capital
  • Health and Nutrition
  • Economic Development
  • Demographics and Population Studies
  • Infrastructure and Urban Planning
  • Environment and Climate

Each dataset in our collection has been carefully selected based on its relevance, quality, and potential impact on policy research and decision-making.

Our Approach

We believe in:

  • Accessibility: Making data available to researchers, policymakers, and practitioners who can use it to create positive change
  • Quality: Ensuring all datasets meet rigorous standards for accuracy and reliability
  • Documentation: Providing comprehensive metadata and documentation for each dataset
  • Responsible Use: Promoting ethical and responsible use of data in research and policy-making
